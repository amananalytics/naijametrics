Primary Research

Statistics final year project at the University of Abuja (supervised by Dr. Rauf, graduating May 2026). Dataset covers 8 occupational categories across Q1–Q3 2024. Descriptive Statistics

χ²
Chi-Square Homogeneity Test
Tests whether the distribution of job creation and separation across occupational categories is statistically homogeneous. Full analysis performed in base R with contingency table output.
🌐
Live Public APIs
Macro data from the World Bank Open Data API via a browser-safe proxy. Live FX rates from Open ER API. Both refresh automatically every 15 minutes while the tab is visible.
